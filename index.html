In Taisho-era Japan, Tanjiro Kamado is a kindhearted boy who makes a living selling charcoal.

However, his peaceful life is shattered when a Demon slaughters his entire family.His little sister Nezuko is the only survivor, but she has been transformed into a Demon herself! Tanjiro sets out on a dangerous journey to find a way to return his sister to normal and destroy the Demon who ruined his life.b

CHARACTERS

Tanjiro Kamado

He is a Demon Slayer in the Demon Slayer Corps, who joined to find a remedy to turn his sister, Nezuko Kamado, back into a human.

Nezuko Kamado

She is a Demon and the younger sister of Tanjiro Kamado and one of the two remaining members of the Kamado family.

Zenitsu Agatsuma

He is also a Demon Slayer in the Demon Slayer Corps and travelling companion of Tanjiro Kamado and Nezuko Kamado.

Inosuke Hashibira

He is also a young man of forest in the Demon Slayer Corps and travelling companion of Tanjiro Kamado and Nezuko Kamado.

Kanao Tsuyuri

She is a Demon Slayer of the Demon Slayer Corps, Shinobu Kocho's Tsuguko and the adopted sister of Shinobu and Kanae Kocho.

Giyu Tomioka

He is a Demon Slayer of the Demon Slayer Corps and the current Water Hashira (水みず柱ばしら Mizu Bashira?).

Shinobu Kocho

She is a Demon Slayer of the Demon Slayer Corps and the current Insect Hashira (蟲むし柱ばしら Mushi Bashira?).

Kyojuro Rengoku

He was a Demon Slayer of the Demon Slayer Corps and the former Flame Hashira (炎えん柱ばしら En Bashira?).

Demon Slayer Corps

The Demon Slayer Corps is an organization that has existed since ancient times, dedicating its existence to protecting humanity from Demons.

There are hundreds of Demon Slayers within the organization, however they are not officially recognized by the government. With Muzan Kibutsuji's demise and the near-extinction of the Demon race, the Corps officially disbanded.

Hashira

The Hashira (Hashira?, lit. Pillars) are the most powerful combatants in the Demon Slayer Corps. As such, they are the organization's top-ranking, most elite warriors who each serve a pivotal role in their war against Muzan Kibutsuji.

The Hashira are the nine overall most powerful members and combatants in the entirety of the Demon Slayer Corps.
