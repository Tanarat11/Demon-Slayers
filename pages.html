Now a member of the Demon Slayer Corps, Tanjiro travels with Nezuko to a town to the northwest where girls have been disappearing night after night. His first assignment is to track down the Demon abducting the girls and eliminate it.

A young man named Kazumi has been blamed for allowing his fiancee to disappear in the latest incident. With Kazumi's help, Tanjiro tracks down a morphed demon with the ability to open portals to its own hidden swamp.

Under a hypnotic suggestion from Sakonji, Nezuko helps Tanjiro protect humans. Tanjiro dives into the kidnapper's bog and defeats two of the three demons there using training from Mt. Sagiri. He interrogates the third and final Demon, only to learn that Muzan Kibutsuji is somehow involved.

Tanjiro learns Muzan is the man who slaughtered the Kamado Family and transformed his sister into a Demon. He bids farewell to Kazumi, who realizes that the boy has lost people just like his fiancee. Shortly after, Tanjiro receives his next mission.

The Asakusa Arc is the third arc of Kimetsu no Yaiba.

Tanjiro ventures to Asakusa, Tokyo for his second mission with the Demon Slayer Corps. The big city of Tokyo is new to the village boy. He is to track down a Demon disturbance in the area, but early into his assignment, Tanjiro notices an all too familiar scent. He frantically rushes into the crowded streets and discovers Muzan Kibutsuji masquerading as a human with a family.

To escape the situation, Muzan transforms a man into a Demon and flees with his family as Tanjiro deals with the new demon. This gains the attention of Lady Tamayo and a young boy named Yushiro. They dispell the situation and bring Tanjiro back to their secret house where Tamayo, a Demon doctor, treats patients. Yushiro is a demon as well, and they're trying to eliminate Muzan.

Meanwhile, Muzan is enraged by Tanjiro's appearance, reminding him of a similar Demon Slayer from the past. He orders two of his followers: Susamaru and Yahaba to bring him Tanjiro's head.

Demon Slayer Corps

The Demon Slayer Corps is an organization that has existed since ancient times, dedicating its existence to protecting humanity from Demons.

There are hundreds of Demon Slayers within the organization, however they are not officially recognized by the government. With Muzan Kibutsuji's demise and the near-extinction of the Demon race, the Corps officially disbanded.

Hashira

The Hashira (Hashira?, lit. Pillars) are the most powerful combatants in the Demon Slayer Corps. As such, they are the organization's top-ranking, most elite warriors who each serve a pivotal role in their war against Muzan Kibutsuji.

The Hashira are the nine overall most powerful members and combatants in the entirety of the Demon Slayer Corps.
